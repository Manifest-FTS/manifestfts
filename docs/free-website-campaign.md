## Current Problem

### 1. Keyword Intent Mismatch

We are targeting broad terms like:
- free website
- create website free
- how much does a website cost
- affordable website

This brings in:
- DIY users (Wix, Squarespace, etc.)
- People looking for tutorials
- People expecting **completely free tools**

But the offer is:
> Service-based, subscription-backed website

---

### 2. Expectation Mismatch

User expectation:
> “Free website builder”

Actual offer:
> “Free setup, $149/month service”

→ This causes **bounce before conversion**

---

### 3. Landing Page Issues (Likely)

From analysis:

- Pricing may not be clear enough above the fold
- Value differentiation may not be immediate
- Not enough trust signals (examples, proof, clarity)
- CTA may come too early without context
